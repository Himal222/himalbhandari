Research

LST analysis of Kathmandu valley

An innovative study utilizing machine learning models to analyze Land Surface Temperature (LST) patterns in the Kathmandu Valley. The research employs satellite imagery and advanced algorithms to identify urban heat islands and assess their relationship with land use changes over time. The findings provide crucial insights for urban planning and climate change mitigation strategies in the rapidly developing valley.

Seismic Analysis of Traditional Buildings

A comprehensive study on the seismic vulnerability of traditional Newari architecture in Nepal. This research focuses on analyzing the structural behavior of historic buildings under seismic loads and proposes innovative retrofitting techniques that preserve cultural heritage while enhancing structural safety.
